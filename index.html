Frequently Asked Questions

Who can become an organ donor?

  • Almost anyone can become an organ donor, regardless of age or medical history.
  • Certain conditions may prevent donation of specific organs.
  • Doctors assess suitability on a case-by-case basis.

How do organ donations save lives?

  • Organ donations can replace failing or damaged organs in patients.
  • A single donor can save up to nine lives by donating multiple organs such as the heart, lungs, kidneys, liver, and pancreas.

How can I register to become an organ donor?

  • You can register online through your country’s organ donation website.
  • Indicate your choice on your driver’s license.
  • It’s also important to inform your family about your decision to donate.
